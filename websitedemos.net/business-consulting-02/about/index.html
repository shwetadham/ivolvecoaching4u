My name is Shweta and I am newly qualified life coach. I am an engineer and MBA by qualification, with over a decade of experience across project management and organisational change management roles in multi-national firms. I am a member of the Association for Coaching, having recently completed my Certification in Coaching from Cambridge University. I am a firm believer in living a fulfilling life and having been through a number of transitions in my professional and personal life, I finally found my true calling... My passion is to empower people who are looking to make transitions in work or life by offering Personal Coaching / Career Coaching. My coaching approach includes clarifying your goals, understanding your values and strengths and driving action / decisions. I tend to use positive psychology, person-centred coaching approaches. I have lived and worked in India and UK, generally with teams spread across the globe, which helps me comprehend the challenges faced by clients who are, or who have been, based / working in a cross-cultural environment.
